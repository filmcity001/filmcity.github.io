Movie Details

F3: Fun and Frustration is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language comedy film written and directed by Anil Ravipudi. Produced by Sri Venkateswara Creations, it is a standalone sequel to the 2019 film F2, and the second film in the Fun and Frustration series. The film stars Venkatesh, Varun Tej, Tamannaah, and Mehreen Pirzada who reprise their roles from the previous film alongside Sunil and Sonal Chauhan. It features music composed by Devi Sri Prasad.
