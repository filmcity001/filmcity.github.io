Movie Details

Demonte Colony is a 2015 Indian Tamil-language horror thriller film[2] written and directed by R Ajay Gnanamuthu. The film stars Arulnithi.[3] Also starring Ramesh Thilak, Sananth and Abishek Joseph George, the film is a fictional story set at the Demonte Colony in Alwarpet, Chennai.[4][5] Demonte Colony features cinematography by Aravinnd singh, songs composed by guitarist Keba Jeremiah,[6] and an original score by S. Chinna. Upon Release, the film garnered positive reviews.
