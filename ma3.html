Directed and written by: Vignesh Shivan Starring by: Vijay Sethupathi, Nayanthara, Samantha Ruth Prabhu Genres: Comedy, Drama, Romance Country: India Language: Malayalam Vijay Sethupathi plays Rambo, who is born in a family that believes they will die if they get married. Rambo’s father dies on the day he’s born. Growing up with this tragedy, Rambo believes that he’s the unluckiest person in the world. As a kid, he believes he’s so unlucky that he could even kill his ailing mom if he comes close to her. Therefore, he runs away from his village and stays as far as possible from his mother. He checks in on her health at regular intervals by staying in touch with his aunt.
